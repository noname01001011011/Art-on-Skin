FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does it hurt?

Pain levels vary depending on placement and individual tolerance. Most clients describe it as manageable discomfort rather than severe pain.

How long does healing take?

Typically 2–4 weeks for surface healing. Complete healing can take 2–3 months. We provide detailed aftercare instructions.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely! We can work with your design or create something custom. Our artists will help refine it for the best tattoo result.

What is the minimum age?

You must be 18 years or older. Valid ID is required for all clients.

How do I prepare for my session?

Get a good night's sleep, eat a proper meal, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol 24 hours before. Wear comfortable clothing.
